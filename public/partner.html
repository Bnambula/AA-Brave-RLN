The Process

From Application to First Payout

01
Apply Online

Fill in the application form with your company details, fleet size, routes, and chosen plan. Takes 5 minutes.

02
Admin Review

Our team reviews your application within 24 hours on business days. We verify your TIN, PSV licence, and fleet details.

03
Account Created

On approval, we create your operator account and configure your modules. You receive login credentials via SMS.

04
Submit Your First Trip

Enter your trip in the Operator Dashboard. Admin approves it and it immediately appears live on the Raylane public website.

05
Passengers Book

Passengers find your coach on raylaneexpress.com, select seats, and pay via MTN MoMo, Airtel, or card.

06
Weekly Payout

Every week, Raylane settles your net earnings (gross minus commission) direct to your MTN MoMo merchant account.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does approval take? +
Most applications are reviewed within 24 hours on weekdays. We will confirm via SMS. Once approved, your dashboard is ready within 2 hours.
What documents do I need to apply? +
You need: Uganda Revenue Authority TIN number, PSV Operator Licence, at least one vehicle registration, at least one PSV-licensed driver, and an MTN MoMo merchant code for payouts.
When do I receive my payouts? +
Payouts are processed weekly every Friday for the previous week's confirmed and completed bookings. Net amount (gross minus commission) is transferred to your MTN MoMo merchant account. You receive an SMS receipt.
Can I run my own buses and still join? +
Absolutely. Raylane works with both private operators and manages its own internal fleet. Your vehicles are managed entirely through your Operator Dashboard. Raylane's own fleet is managed separately by Admin.
Can I upgrade my plan later? +
Yes. You can upgrade your plan at any time from your Dashboard Settings tab. Admin activates the new modules within 24 hours of payment confirmation. You can also add individual modules without upgrading your full plan.
How does parcel revenue work for operators? +
When a passenger sends a parcel on your bus, you earn the parcel fee minus Raylane's 8% commission. This is automatically calculated and included in your weekly payout. At scale, this can add UGX 2–5M per month in additional revenue.
What happens if a passenger cancels? +
Standard bookings cancelled more than 6 hours before departure receive an 80% refund. The 20% handling fee is shared between Raylane and the operator. Advance bookings cancelled 48+ hours before departure receive a full refund of the commitment fee.
