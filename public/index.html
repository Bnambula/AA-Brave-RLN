Simple Process

Book Your Coach in 4 Steps

From search to QR ticket in under 60 seconds. No account required, no app to download.

01
Search Your Route

Enter your departure city, destination, date, and passenger count. See all available coaches with real-time seat counts and pricing.

02
Choose Your Exact Seat

Our interactive coach layout shows exactly which seats are available. Pick window, aisle, front, or back — your choice is guaranteed.

03
Pay via Mobile Money

MTN MoMo or Airtel Money sends a USSD push directly to your phone. Enter your PIN. Payment confirmed in ~8 seconds. No smartphone needed.

04
Board with QR Ticket

Your QR ticket arrives via SMS instantly. Show it to the driver at the bay. They scan it — green screen means you're on board.

🔒 How Mobile Money Payment Works
1. Enter your MTN or Airtel number at checkout
2. You receive a USSD push on your phone — any phone, including 2G feature phones
3. Enter your Mobile Money PIN — secure, no sharing with us
4. Confirmation arrives in approximately 8 seconds
5. Your QR ticket is generated and sent via SMS instantly
